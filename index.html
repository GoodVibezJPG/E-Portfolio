About Me

ÍY SȻÁĆEL (Good day in SENĆOŦEN)!

As a proud W̱SÁNEĆ Tswout from the Saanich Peninsula, I am a first-year student at Camosun College dedicated to reconnecting with my Indigenous roots and contributing to my community. My journey brings me back to Victoria not just as a student of technology, but as a member of the W̱SÁNEĆ Nation committed to bridging traditional knowledge with modern technological innovation.

My path is driven by a deep desire to support and empower my community through education and technical expertise. The land, my community, and the potential of technology are my greatest inspirations. Through my studies and work, I hope to create pathways that honor both traditional wisdom and technological advancement, serving as a bridge between generations and cultures.
